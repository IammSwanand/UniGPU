# 1. Introduction

Welcome to **UniGPU** ("**UniGPU**", "**we**", "**our**", or "**us**").

UniGPU operates a distributed GPU computing platform that enables individuals and organizations to access, provide, and manage computing resources for artificial intelligence, machine learning, rendering, scientific computing, software development, and other compute-intensive workloads.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information when you access or use the UniGPU website, applications, APIs, dashboards, Provider Agent, marketplace, enterprise services, and any related services (collectively, the "**Services**").

By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
