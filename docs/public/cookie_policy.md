# 3. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website.

Cookies help websites:

- Remember user preferences.
- Maintain authenticated sessions.
- Improve website functionality.
- Enhance security.
- Measure performance.
- Analyze platform usage.

UniGPU may also use similar technologies such as local storage, session storage, and other browser-based technologies where appropriate.

Instructions for managing cookies can usually be found in your browser's help documentation.
