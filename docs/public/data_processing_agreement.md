# 1. Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between UniGPU ("Processor") and the Customer ("Controller") where UniGPU processes Personal Data on behalf of the Customer while providing the Services.

This DPA supplements the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Enterprise Agreement (if applicable), and other agreements governing the Services.

---

# 2. Definitions

Capitalized terms used in this DPA have the meanings assigned to them in the **Legal Definitions** document unless expressly defined otherwise.

For purposes of this DPA:

**Controller** means the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data.

**Processor** means the entity that processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.

**Personal Data** means information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under applicable data protection laws.

**Applicable Data Protection Law** means any privacy or data protection legislation applicable to the processing of Personal Data.

---

# 3. Scope

This DPA applies only where UniGPU processes Personal Data on behalf of the Customer while providing the Services.

Examples include:

- Account information
- Authentication information
- Billing information
- Organization management
- Support requests
- User metadata
- Operational logs containing Personal Data

This DPA does not apply to processing activities where UniGPU acts as an independent Controller.

---

# 4. Roles of the Parties

For Customer Personal Data processed through the Services:

- Customer acts as the Controller.
- UniGPU acts as the Processor.

The Customer determines:

- Why Personal Data is processed.
- Which Personal Data is uploaded.
- How Personal Data is used within Customer workloads.

UniGPU processes Personal Data solely for the purpose of providing the Services.

---

# 5. Processing of Personal Data

UniGPU shall process Personal Data only:

- On documented instructions from the Customer.
- To provide the Services.
- To comply with applicable law.
- To protect the security and integrity of the Services.

UniGPU shall not sell Customer Personal Data.

UniGPU shall not use Customer Personal Data for advertising purposes.

---

# 6. Customer Responsibilities

The Customer is responsible for:

- Obtaining all necessary permissions.
- Providing required privacy notices.
- Ensuring a lawful basis for processing.
- Determining appropriate retention periods.
- Ensuring uploaded workloads comply with applicable law.

The Customer remains responsible for the content of Compute Jobs and User Content.

---

# 7. UniGPU Responsibilities

UniGPU shall:

- Process Personal Data only as authorized.
- Maintain reasonable security measures.
- Restrict access to authorized personnel.
- Ensure personnel handling Personal Data are subject to confidentiality obligations.
- Assist Customers where reasonably practicable in fulfilling data protection obligations.

---

# 8. Confidentiality

UniGPU shall ensure that employees, contractors, and authorized personnel with access to Personal Data are subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

Access to Personal Data shall be limited to personnel who require such access to provide the Services.

---

# 9. Security Measures

UniGPU maintains reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect Personal Data.

Security measures may include:

- Encryption in transit.
- Encryption at rest where appropriate.
- Role-based access controls.
- Authentication systems.
- Infrastructure monitoring.
- Audit logging.
- Secure software deployment.
- Incident response procedures.

Security measures may evolve as the Services develop.

---

# 10. Subprocessors

UniGPU may engage trusted subprocessors to assist in providing the Services.

Examples may include:

- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Payment processors
- Email delivery providers
- Authentication providers
- Customer support providers

UniGPU will take commercially reasonable steps to ensure subprocessors are subject to appropriate contractual obligations relating to Personal Data.

A current list of subprocessors may be provided upon reasonable request or published separately.

---

# 11. International Data Transfers

Customer Personal Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside the Customer's country.

Where required by applicable law, UniGPU will implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers of Personal Data.

---

# 12. Data Subject Rights

Where reasonably practicable and taking into account the nature of the processing, UniGPU will assist Customers in responding to requests relating to:

- Access
- Correction
- Deletion
- Restriction
- Portability
- Objection to processing

Customers remain responsible for responding to requests from their own users.

---

# 13. Personal Data Breach

If UniGPU becomes aware of a Personal Data Breach affecting Customer Personal Data, UniGPU will make commercially reasonable efforts to:

- Investigate the incident.
- Contain the incident.
- Mitigate potential harm.
- Notify affected Customers where required by applicable law.
- Provide information reasonably necessary to support Customer compliance obligations.

---

# 14. Data Retention & Deletion

UniGPU retains Personal Data only for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the Services.
- Comply with legal obligations.
- Resolve disputes.
- Protect the security of the platform.

Upon termination of the Services, Customer Personal Data will be deleted or anonymized in accordance with UniGPU's retention practices, unless continued retention is required by law.

---

# 15. Audits

Upon reasonable written request, UniGPU may provide Customers with information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA.

Nothing in this DPA requires UniGPU to disclose:

- Trade secrets.
- Confidential security information.
- Proprietary infrastructure details.
- Information affecting the security of other Customers.

---

# 16. Liability

The liability of each party under this DPA shall be subject to the limitations of liability contained in the applicable Terms of Service or Enterprise Agreement unless otherwise required by applicable law.

---

# 17. Changes to this Agreement

UniGPU may update this DPA from time to time.

Material changes will be communicated in accordance with applicable agreements.

Continued use of the Services after such changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised DPA.
